# Route Survey Note

Candidate routes considered in the seeded workflow:

1. Direct nitration of anisole with nitric acid, which is short but strongly exothermic and harder to control.
2. Methoxylation of p-nitrochlorobenzene with methanol and sodium hydroxide, which offers better downstream handling and clearer separation logic.
3. Etherification of p-nitrophenol with methanol, which is plausible but less attractive on yield and separation burden.

The seeded implementation prefers route 2 because it fits a home-paper style conceptual design well and yields a credible process train with filtration, distillation, evaporation, and storage.
