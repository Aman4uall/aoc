India siting note for a high-capacity ethylene glycol plant:

- Gujarat clusters such as Dahej and Jamnagar are attractive because of petrochemical integration, marine logistics, and industrial utility infrastructure.
- Dahej is strong on chemical-cluster services, west-coast port connectivity, and established operating ecosystem.
- Jamnagar benefits from refining and petrochemical integration but project-specific utility tie-ins still need confirmation.
- Paradip offers east-coast port access and strategic logistics, but the surrounding petrochemical services ecosystem is generally thinner than the major Gujarat clusters.

Site selection logic:
- Feedstock integration matters more than simple land cost.
- Utility reliability, wastewater handling, and export connectivity should be included in the site score.
- For an India-only feasibility package, site justification should remain tied to public Indian industrial references and tariff context.
